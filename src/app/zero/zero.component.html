Regular Expressions

  • Two main rules
    • The earliest (leftmost) match wins
    • Standard quantifiers are greedy
  • NFA Engines (Details Right side →)
    • Traditional NFA
    • POSIX (always picks the longest of the leftmode matches.
      • Example: cat|category Would match "category" even if "cat" appears earlier.
  • Regular Expression Engines
    • DFA: Deterministic Finite Automation
    • NFA: Nondeterministic Finite Automation
        • Slower than DFA but:
      • Capturing, Look-Around and NonGreedy Quantifiers
